HealthConnect

Madiba HealthConnect is a platform committed to making healthcare accessible and convenient for all students and staff. Our clinic booking platform allows you to manually book a slot for in-person consultations or choose a tele-consultation with a professional nurse through triage booking. Whether you need routine care or urgent support, our system is designed to be user-friendly and efficient. We believe that everyone deserves timely and compassionate medical attention, and our platform is here to help you get the care you need, when you need it.

Tele-Consult

Our tele-consultation service connects you with qualified healthcare professionals from the comfort of your own space. Through secure video calls and chat, you can discuss your health concerns, receive medical advice, and get prescriptions when necessary. This service is perfect for non-emergency consultations, follow-up appointments, and general health inquiries. Available 24/7 to ensure you get the support you need at any time.

Manual Booking

Schedule your in-person appointments at our campus clinic through our easy-to-use booking system. Choose from available time slots that work with your academic schedule, select your preferred healthcare provider, and specify the type of consultation you need. You can also reschedule or cancel appointments as needed. Our booking system ensures efficient clinic operations and minimal waiting times.

Health News

Stay informed about the latest health updates, wellness tips, and important announcements from our healthcare team. Get access to articles about preventive care, mental health resources, vaccination schedules, and campus health initiatives. We regularly update our content to keep the university community informed about health matters that affect students and staff.
